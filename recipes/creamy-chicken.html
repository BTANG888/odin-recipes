Creamy Pan Chicken

Description

Calling all garlic lovers! This creamy garlic chicken is an easy, fragrant skillet dish that is great with mashed potatoes or mashed cauliflower. Since it cooks in one pan, cleanup is easy, too

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon italian herb seasoning, or sesasoning blend of choice
  • 1/2 Teaspoon, salt to taste
  • 1/4 Teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flower
  • 2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken thights
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 garlic cloves, or more as needed, peeled and smashed, about 1/4 cup lightly packed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or as needed

Directions to cook chicken

  1. Combine herb seasoning, salt, pepper, granulated garlic, and flour in a 1 gallon resealable plastic bag and mix well
  2. Comebine butter and olive oil in a large, nonstick skjillet over medium heat. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towelsm place in the bag of seasoned flour, seal, and toss hicken thighs in the flour mixture until evenly coated.
  3. When butter stops sizzling, add chicken pieces and cook until nicely browned, no longer pink at the center and juices run clear, 4 to 5 mins per side. An instant read thermometer inserted near the center should read 165 degrees F. Remove to a plate and keep warm.
  4. Add smashed garlic to the same skillet. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook and cook garlic, stirring frequently, just until gragrant, no longer than 1 minute.
  5. Add cream to the skillet, and stir up any browned bits in the bottom of the pan. Stir in chicken broth. Increase heat to medium, and bring the sauce to a boil, stirring occasionaly. Cook until the sauce is reduced by half, about 10 minutes
